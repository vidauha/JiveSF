J.I.V.E. BABY - by Joanne Liu

Whenever I tell people about Jive, their first response is, “Cool! I’ll check out DriveSF.” “No, no, it’s Jive,” I explain. Joining In Virtuous Endeavors. Cheesy acronym? Sure is, but that’s what I love about Jive. We get to do whatever we want, from meaningful volunteer events to meaningful large-scale parties! The concept of Jive was formulated in a lazy car ride along the 101 with my roommate Vida. I was able to tap into my pool of brilliant SF friends who all bring something unique to the table. They all have initiative, which was most important. But please allow me to describe each officer in a way the Officers webpage cannot.

Vida has incredible energy and fears nothing. She will shake her finger at you like nobody’s business. Cindy has the sincerest love for serving the community. Greggy Poo is our ever peppy mascot, while Shaun is the too cool for school guy who yells, “JIVE BABY!” out the window whenever we finish an event. Reliable Dave updates our website every 10 minutes and talks like an announcer. Sweet Lindsey heads up recruiting while Steve balances her out by maintaining the “ratio.” Erwin constantly dreams about our funds (or our lack thereof), and Julie is always eager to dip her hands into any project. Michelle is tall, loud, and types up the meanest minutes. This group is the group who did not roll their eyes when the idea of Jive was discussed at the dinner table. This group is incredible. I suppose it is only fair that I comment on the final unmentioned member. Joanne is…well…I’m hungry.

Founded in October ‘04, JiveSF is in the process of filing for non-profit status. Our primary purpose is to unite young professionals in contributing to and serving the community. .Volunteer work can be fun, and we hope to remind everyone of that.

Sincerest thanks,

Joanne
