Why Volunteering? - by Cindy Fang

Greg asked me at the last JIVE gathering, "Why do you like to volunteer?" I explained to him that I have realized that life is so much more meaningful to me when I focus on others and recognize that there are many great causes that need our help and support.

Most recently, JIVE helped the children at the Hamilton Family Center (HFC) go back-to-school shopping at Mervyn's! We had a good time helping the kids pick out over $1,200 in new clothes and shoes! Everyone was so excited and happy to have Mervyn's donate to these underprivileged children. We hope to participate in more events with the youth at HFC.

JIVE continues to host Friday night BINGO at the Marian Residence for Women. The women are always excited to play BINGO and win prizes that we bring for them. As we continue our involvement there, the women at Marian become more comfortable with us and have slowly opened up. It is truly moving to see women begin to share their lives with us. We plan to continue our monthly volunteering at Marian through the end of the year.

For those who do not have time to volunteer, they can still contribute to the community through their donations at our social events! This year, JIVE has raised over $6,000 for charity, benefiting local and national charities and organizations, including the TNDC, YMCA, the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society, Edgewood Center for Children, and East Palo Alto Cesar Chavez Middle School.

Thank you to those who have participated in our community service events. Of course, we always welcome your ideas and suggestions, and if you'd like to help organize a service event with JIVE, please contact Cindy cfang329@yahoo.com. Stay tuned for upcoming events - Support the fight against Breast Cancer at Race for the Cure and save our environment at our Tree Planting in SF neighborhoods. Next month JIVE plans to return volunteer at the SF Food Bank. Last time, we sorted hundreds of pounds of oranges and frozen corn! :)
